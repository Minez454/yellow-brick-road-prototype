Yellow Brick Road is a dual-sided digital ecosystem designed to bridge the critical gap between homeless individuals ("Members") and social service providers ("Agents") in Las Vegas. 

Unlike static directories, this platform operates as a **Live Command Center**, providing real-time data on shelter bed availability, food resource status, and case management.

### 3. The Academy (School of Life)
A personalized curriculum generator that addresses the **"Survival to Maintenance"** transition.
* **Diagnostic Tree:** Builds a unique lesson plan based on user inputs (e.g., "Do you have a pet?", "Do you cook on an open fire?").
* **Life Skills Modules:** Covers critical gaps often overlooked, such as *Fire Safety for Tents*, *Trench Foot Prevention*, and *Indoor Re-integration Psychology*.
* **Creative Studio:** Built-in art therapy tools for mental health decompression.

### 4. Secure Digital Vault
An encrypted wallet for storing critical identity documents (State IDs, Birth Certificates, Housing Applications), ensuring users never lose progress due to lost physical paperwork.

### 5. Dual-Sided Architecture
The platform features two distinct interfaces:
* **Member Portal:** For individuals seeking aid.
* **Agent Portal:** A "Back Office" for agencies (e.g., The Shade Tree, NPHY) to update bed counts in real-time, manage waitlists, and conduct virtual intakes via Zoom.
